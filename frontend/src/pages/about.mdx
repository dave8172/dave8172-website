## Hi, I'm <RandomColor>Dave</RandomColor>

I build trustworthy AI production systems — specifically for finance and accounting workflows.

My focus is on the gap between "it works in the demo" and "we can hand this to an auditor." That gap is where most AI data pipelines live, and closing it is the work I find most interesting.

---

## What I build

- **Trust-retrofit for AI extraction pipelines** — labeled eval sets, accuracy and failure reports, confidence scoring, audit logs, PII redaction. The layer that makes an existing pipeline something you can actually stand behind.
- **Document processing pipelines** — structured extraction from invoices, bank statements, receipts, and whatever other documents your back-office runs on. Built in Python, not low-code wrappers.
- **AI benchmarking and model evaluation** — does GPT-4o or a specialized OCR API actually fit your use case? I'll answer that with numbers, not opinions.
- **Secure, self-hostable extraction kits** — designed for environments where you can't send financial documents to a third-party API.

The common thread: I care about what happens after the demo. Reliability, auditability, and security are the defaults — not the add-ons you bolt on when something breaks.

---

## Who I work with

Mid-market finance teams, CA firms, and fintech back-office operations that are building on AI and finding that "good enough" isn't good enough when the data is financial. Usually they have an extraction pipeline that works most of the time — and they need to know what it's doing the rest of the time.
