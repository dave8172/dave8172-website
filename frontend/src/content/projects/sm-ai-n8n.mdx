## The Logic: From Link to Live Post

The system is designed to be <RandomColor>invisible</RandomColor> to the user, requiring only two "touchpoints":

1.  **The Trigger:** The owner pastes a product URL or a blog link into a Google Sheet.
2.  **The AI Analysis:** My custom Python worker scrapes the website to understand the product context, price, and benefits—generating a platform-specific caption (X, LinkedIn, Instagram).
3.  **Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) Verification:** The owner receives an <RandomColor>automated email</RandomColor> with the draft. They can click **"Approve"** to post instantly or **"Edit"** to make a quick tweak.
4.  **Auto-Logging:** Once posted, the system logs the live URL back into the Google Sheet for easy tracking and reporting.
