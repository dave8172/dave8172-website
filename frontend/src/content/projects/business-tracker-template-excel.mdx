## Overview

In the world of small business, <RandomColor>speed and clarity</RandomColor> are everything. This Business Tracker Template is built for the small businesses 
which needs professional insights without the complexity. It turns daily transactions into an interactive dashboard instantly.

Simply enter your data through streamlined dropdowns, and the template handles the rest—calculating taxes, updating stock levels, and 
generating clean, insightful visuals so you can monitor your business health at a glance.

## Key Features
- **Intelligent Dashboard:** View your business health at a glance with a two-fold system: KPI cards on top and interactive charts below, including 
<RandomColor>slicers</RandomColor> to filter charts based on categories, time, etc.

- **Advanced Data Modeling with Power Pivot:** Experience lightning-fast performance and complex data relationships. 
By utilizing Power Pivot, this template <RandomColor>manages large datasets</RandomColor> and multi-table relationships (Data Modeling) that traditional Excel sheets simply can’t handle.

- **Rapid Data Entry Ledger:** Save time with a streamlined transaction log featuring built-in product dropdowns and smart lookups that 
<RandomColor>automatically calculate</RandomColor> prices, taxes and totals for you.

- **Live Stock Management:** Never miss a sale with a dedicated Inventory sheet that monitors Stock In vs. Stock Out and <RandomColor>flags low-inventory</RandomColor> 
items with automated "REORDER" alerts.

- **Automated Tax Engine:** Simplify your accounting with a system that handles multiple tax tiers and provides 
instant summaries of your tax liabilities.
