## Overview

In the world of small business, <RandomColor>speed and clarity</RandomColor> are everything. This Business Tracker Template is built for the small businesses 
which needs professional insights without the complexity. It turns daily transactions into an interactive dashboard instantly.

Simply enter your data through a <RandomColor>simple invoicing system</RandomColor>, with forms + button UI, and the template handles the rest,
- calculating taxes, updating transaction log, stock levels, and 
- generating clean, insightful visuals so you can monitor your business health at a glance.

There are Product and Vendor Master sheets to easily add new data and modify existing ones. Previous invoices can be quickly fetched
from the transaction log using the invoice number, allowing you to easily reprint or review past transactions.

This is a template, which can be <RandomColor>quickly customised</RandomColor> as per your business needs.

## Key Features
- **Intelligent Dashboard:** View your business health at a glance with a two-fold system: KPI cards on top and interactive charts below, including 
<RandomColor>slicers</RandomColor> to filter charts based on categories, time, etc.

- **Simple, clean invoicing system:** Non-technical users can easily create, save and fetch invoices using a <RandomColor>user-friendly form interface.</RandomColor> 
Products can be selected from dropdowns, enter the quantity and the template will automatically calculate prices, taxes and totals.

- **Product and Vendor Master Sheets** Save time with a <RandomColor>streamlined</RandomColor> mini-database for your products and vendors. 
Easily add new items or update existing ones, and watch as your invoices and inventory adjust in real-time.

- **Live Stock Management:** Never miss a sale with a dedicated Inventory sheet that monitors Stock In vs. Stock Out and <RandomColor>flags low-inventory</RandomColor> 
items with automated "REORDER" alerts.

- **Automated Tax Engine:** Simplify your accounting with a system that handles multiple tax tiers and provides 
instant summaries of your tax liabilities.
