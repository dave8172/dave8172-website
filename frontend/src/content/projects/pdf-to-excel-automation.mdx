## Python Libraries & Tools

- <RandomColor>pdfplumber:</RandomColor> For high-precision extraction of metadata, headers, and positioned text (non-OCR).

- <RandomColor>Camelot (Lattice & Stream):</RandomColor> For complex table extraction. Camelot is industry-leading for detecting table structures, borders, 
and multi-line cell data that standard tools miss.

- <RandomColor>pytesseract (OCR):</RandomColor> To handle non-digital, scanned, or image-based PDFs. I use Tesseract to convert "unreadable" scans into searchable 
data, ensuring no invoice is left unprocessed.

- <RandomColor>Openpyxl:</RandomColor> To programmatically build your Excel template, including merged cells, custom borders, shaded headers, and A4 
print-ready formatting.
